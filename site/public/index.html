About Me

I believe that every person deserves the opportunity to be able to connect with nature and I recognize that every interaction I have may be the inspiration that leads to this. I understand that different people will connect with nature in different ways, however, I believe that the world around us can provide everybody with the opportunity to have a meaningful interaction with nature.

Being passionate about the environment, I have volunteered for both the Avon and Wiltshire Wildlife Trusts, completed a Geography degree and will often be found outdoors either walking or playing sports. Other hobbies of mine include working on self-designed sewing projects, learning to like cooking and photography.

Right now I am improving my flora and fauna identification skills and learning more about the habitats around me and our impact on them. I would ideally like a role where I make a difference by connecting people with nature, protect the environment for the future and undertake continued professional and personal development.
